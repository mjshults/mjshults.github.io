Career

Cherry Republic

I first became interested in Marketing through my experience as an intern at Cherry Republic located in Northern Michigan. Through this internship I discovered my interest in working in teams, using my creative skills, developing strategies and executing these plans. I became interested in the interactive and creative side of business.

Uline

Last summer, I worked as a Marketing Intern for Uline. This opporunity allowed me to understand the market research and analytical side of Marketing. At Uline, I worked closely with vendors, analyzed customer return reports, completed quality audits, and conducted competitor reviews for specific product lines. It was at Uline that I discovered my passion for applying my research and analytical skills to my work.

Extracurriculars

Beyond this internship, I have pursued leadership roles in my extracurriculars that allow me to practice my marketing and networking skills by planning and marketing events to the student body and working with local businesses to fundraise for such events.

Career Goals

Marketing allows me to combine my passions of both creativity in the workplace and analytical research. In the future, I hope to pursue a master's program in International Marketing in order to get a step closer towards my dream career of being a brand manager for an international company.
